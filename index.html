About Us

Who We Are

Limestone Value Alliance Plc is a proudly Nigerian industrial company delivering complete limestone value-chain solutions — from quarry to finished products.

We mine, process, refine, manufacture, package, and distribute limestone and limestone-derived products for industrial, agricultural, construction, and environmental markets.

Our strength lies in vertical integration, enabling us to control every stage of production to ensure quality, efficiency, reliability, and competitive pricing.

Our Vision

To become a leading African building materials and industrial minerals brand serving global markets.

Our Mission

To deliver consistent, high-quality limestone solutions while creating sustainable value for customers, partners, communities, and shareholders.

Operations & Capabilities

Our integrated operations include limestone quarries, processing plants, kilns, mills, blending units, packaging lines, warehouses, and logistics systems located within Nigeria.

These facilities are designed to ensure efficient extraction, consistent quality, scalable production, and reliable supply to both domestic and international markets.
